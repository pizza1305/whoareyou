CHOCOLATE CAKE

  • Chocolate Truffle Cake
  • Ingredients:
  1. Chocolate
  2. Baking Powder
  3. Flour
  4. Oil
  5. Milk
  6. Chocolate Essence
  7. Sugar
  • Procedure
  1. Take a bowl and add Chocolate,Oil,Vanilla Essence,Sugar and beat it.
  2. To the mix,add Flour and Milk and beat it until you get a thick batter.
  3. Pour the batter into the mould and bake it.
